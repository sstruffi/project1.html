Annabelle Re-brand

This project reimagines Annabelle Cosmetics by evaluating its existing branding and audience. After taking a look at what different competitors are doing and what's working, I got to see exactly what needs work on.

I identified key visual and messaging gaps and responded with a more cohesive brand identity focused on simplicity and everyday confidence. The outcome includes a refreshed visual system and a social campaign concept designed to modernize the brand while staying accessible.
